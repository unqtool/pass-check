Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 11, 2025

Pass Check ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Pass Check website (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Data Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal data. Our password analyzer tool runs entirely on your local device (client-side) in your browser. Any password or data you enter into the tool is never sent to our servers, stored, or logged.

Log Data

Like most website operators, we may collect standard log information that your browser sends whenever you visit our Service ("Log Data"). This Log Data may include information such as your computer's IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit, but it is not linked to any personal information.

Cookies and Third-Party Advertising

We use third-party advertising companies, such as Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies to collect information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies (like the DoubleClick cookie) enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/n or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

About Us

Welcome to Pass Check, your free, fast, and secure tool for password analysis.

Our mission is to provide an easy-to-use utility that helps you understand the strength of your passwords and generate new, robust ones. In an age of constant data breaches, a strong password is your first line of defense.

This tool is 100% free, runs entirely in your browser (client-side), and we do not store, save, or transmit any password you type. Your security and privacy are our top priority.
